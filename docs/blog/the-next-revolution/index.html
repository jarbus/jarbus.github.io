THE NEXT REVOLUTION

I think the AI boom is starting to lose its momentum, but I think the next tech revolution is just around the corner. In China, it’s already arrived–the West is just late to the party.

Everyone should read “Why every country needs to master the Electric Tech Stack” by Noah Smith (it’s about a five minute read). Basically, the claim is that drones are the future of war, and if a country is to secure its national security, it must have a strong domestic supply chain to produce the components needed for drones. This includes:

  1. Lithium-ion batteries, for power
  2. Permanent-magnet electric motors, to make the propellers move efficiently
  3. Power electronics to turn power from the battery into a form the motors can use
  4. Computer chips

These components are also needed for electric vehicles, robots, and subsets are needed for smartphones, data-centers, appliances, and more. Let’s throw in solar, since it’s the cheapest source of power available and isn’t prone to shortages, so it’s a great form of energy security.

Everything will be electric, not for environmental purposes, but because the technology has gotten really, really good. This has all been said many times.

What makes this exciting is that if you understand electronics, you’ll be able to understand pretty much all technology.
